# Hide Progress Demo

A demonstration of hiding the progress bar on specific slides.

---

# Normal Slide

This slide shows the progress bar at the bottom.

You can see the progress indicator showing current slide position.

---

<!-- hideprogress -->

# Hidden Progress

This slide hides the progress bar.

Useful for:
- Transition slides
- Break slides
- Q&A sessions
- Thank you pages

---

# Back to Normal

This slide shows the progress bar again.

The progress bar automatically reappears on slides without the hideprogress directive.

---

<!-- hideprogress -->

# Another Hidden Slide

You can use the hideprogress directive on any slide.

Just add `<!-- hideprogress -->` at the beginning of the slide.

---

# The End

Progress bar is visible again.

Use `<!-- hideprogress -->` to create cleaner slides when needed!
